Fruit and Yoghurt Recipe

Description

recipe for fruit and yoghurt is a quick and easy mix of a few ingredients to create a nice thick and sweet smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine Strawberries, banana, yogurt, orange juice, sugar and milk
  2. Poor into blender and blend until smooth
