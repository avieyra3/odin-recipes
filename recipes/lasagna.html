Lasagna

Description

This alltime favorite lasagna recipe takes a host of ingredients and roughly 3 hours total of time to prepare and cook as a homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • Sweet italian sausage and ground beef
  • 1 onion and two cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tbs of white sugar
  • Seasoned with fresh parsely, dried basel leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Store bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Step-By-Step Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
